Jellyfish July

One of the sea creatures that is a crucial part of that biodiversity is the jellyfish. However, historically, Hong Kong's jellyfish research is limited, with most records dating from the 1980s and 1990s. The territory's tropical location near highly biodiverse waters suggests its jellyfish diversity is probably underestimated.  This series of posts aims to inspire awareness about jellyfish varieties and their crucial role in the seas around us. Starting in July – Jellyfish July – we’ll be featuring one species per month for the following year.
